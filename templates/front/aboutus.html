About Delta

Delta Cancer Screening and Treatment Center is a medical facility located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The center is dedicated to providing comprehensive cancer screening and treatment services to the people of Ethiopia. Delta Cancer Screening and Treatment Center is affiliated with Addis Ababa University, one of the leading universities in Ethiopia, and is staffed by a team of highly trained medical professionals.

At Delta Cancer Screening and Treatment Center, we believe that early detection is key to fighting cancer. That’s why we offer a range of screening services, including mammograms, colonoscopies, and pap smears, to help detect cancer in its early stages. Our team of medical professionals is dedicated to providing the highest quality care to our patients, and we work closely with each patient to develop a personalized treatment plan that meets their unique needs.
